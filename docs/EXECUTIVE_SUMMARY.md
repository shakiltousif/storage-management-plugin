### 4 Custom Tables
1. **wp_royal_storage_units** - Storage unit inventory
2. **wp_royal_parking_spaces** - Parking space inventory
3. **wp_royal_bookings** - Booking records
4. **wp_royal_invoices** - Invoice records

2. **rs_parking_space** - Parking spaces
